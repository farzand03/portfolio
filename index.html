About Me

Hello, I'm Farzan, a third-year electrical engineering student with a passion for crafting websites and web applications from the ground up using various programming languages. Self-taught in this domain, I've embarked on a journey of continuous learning and exploration, improving my skills in web development with each project undertaken.

Alongside my university studies, I've had the privilege of working as an intern engineer in various different departments at BHP, where I've gained firsthand experience in applying theoretical knowledge to real-world projects. This experience has provided me with invaluable insights into the practical aspects of electrical engineering, allowing me to enhance my problem-solving abilities and technical proficiency.

Beyond my academic and professional pursuits, I find immense fulfillment in giving back to my community through volunteering. Currently, I devote my time to Save the Children, contributing to their noble cause with dedication and compassion. Additionally, I serve as a STEM study buddy, sharing my knowledge and enthusiasm for science, technology, engineering, and mathematics with students eager to explore these fields.

In essence, I am a driven individual fueled by curiosity, creativity, and a desire to make a positive impact in the world through technical excellence.
